Data & Privacy
How we handle your data

Google Sign-In Data

Our apps use Google Sign-In for user authentication. When you sign in, we access the following Google user data:

  • Email address — Used to verify your identity and manage your app license
  • Display name — Shown in the app for personalization
  • Profile picture — Displayed in the app for your profile

This data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to any external server. It is used exclusively for authentication and license verification within the app.

Application Data

All personal and financial data you enter into our apps (loans, borrowers, transactions, expenses) is stored entirely on your device using a local SQLite database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device — no cloud sync, no backups to external servers
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Is never used for analytics or advertising

No Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking pixels. We have no way to see how you use the app or what data you enter.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete All Data). Uninstalling the app also permanently removes all data from your device.

For full details, read our complete Privacy Policy.
